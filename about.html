Kyoto VR MQP Team Blog

About

We are a team of three students from Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, Massachusetts working on an augmented reality project in Kyoto, Japan. We're working with Kyoto VR and Ritsumeikan University to develop an AR platform for art and culture.

We'll be staying in Kyoto for twelve months, from mid-July to mid-October to complete this project as part of our graduation requirements. WPI requires all students to complete a largely independent project called the Major Qualifying Project, or MQP, in senior year.
